Our Flavorful Journey

At Damascus Bites, we are passionate about bringing the true essence of Syrian Shawarma to Canada. With over 30 years of culinary expertise, our founder, Kareem Al-Fayed, set out to introduce the bold flavors of the Middle East to food lovers. What started as a small eatery in a cozy corner of Toronto quickly became a go-to spot for its fresh ingredients and authentic taste. Today, we continue this tradition, serving perfectly seasoned shawarmas with a commitment to quality, flavor, and exceptional service.
